Workshops and Webinars

Below is a list of currently scheduled workshops and webinars.

Webinars for nonprofits are $50 per webinar. Unless otherwise indicated, the registration fee for each in-person workshop is $75/person; $60/person if we receive payment at least two business days in advance. Representatives of current New York Foundation grantees may register for many of these training events at no charge.

CLE trainings are $100 per CLE hour. See each listing for specifics.

While Lawyers Alliance mostly offers trainings in a one-hour webinar format, we also hold some longer virtual and in-person sessions.
